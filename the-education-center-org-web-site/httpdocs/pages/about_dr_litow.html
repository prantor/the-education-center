Dr. Michael Litow is the founder and Executive Director of The Education Center of Developmental Resources. He has been affiliated for 30 years with Chicago Public Schools as a teacher, coordinator, assistant principal and consultant. A nationally acclaimed educator and counselor, he has been recognized by many institutions and organizations including the Chicago Jaycees, Chicago area law enforcement agencies, Illinois House of Representatives, Professional Women's Club, the Chicago field office of Phi Delta Kappa, the U.S. Department of Justice and the White House Conference on School Safety. Dr. Litow's straightforward, no-nonsense approach can be seen through some his favorite statements which have come to be known as litowisms.
